Scope

This policy applies to the ReadBooster browser extension and the ReadBooster public website. ReadBooster is designed to turn supported AI conversations into structured, readable document views.

Plain-language summary: the extension reads the conversation page on your device so it can build the reader view. It does not automatically transmit your chat content to a ReadBooster server.

What page data ReadBooster accesses

When you activate ReadBooster on a supported AI platform, the extension accesses page content needed to create the document presentation. Depending on the conversation, this can include:

  • your visible prompts and the AI responses;
  • headings, paragraphs, lists, code blocks, links, citations, images, and tables;
  • the order and structure of conversation sections; and
  • basic page context needed to identify the supported platform and conversation.

ReadBooster processes this page content to render its reading interface. It would be inaccurate to say that no data is processed; the relevant conversation content is processed locally in the browser.

Local processing

Conversation extraction, formatting, outline generation, and reader presentation happen on the device where the browser extension is running. ReadBooster does not automatically send the extracted conversation content to a ReadBooster-operated server.

The original AI platform continues to operate under its own terms and privacy policy. ReadBooster does not control how that platform processes the conversation you have with it.

Local settings storage

ReadBooster may store preferences in browser-provided storage so your reading setup can be restored. These preferences can include theme, typography, spacing, reading-mode, and table-display choices.

Custom section titles or similar reader-view preferences may also be retained locally when the feature requires it. These values support the extension interface; they do not change the original conversation on the AI platform.

Tally feedback

ReadBooster includes a link to an external feedback form provided by Tally. The form is not submitted automatically. Feedback is sent only after you deliberately open the form, choose what to include, and submit it.

Information submitted through that form is processed by Tally under Tally’s own privacy terms. Do not include conversation text, personal information, confidential material, or screenshots unless you intend to share them for feedback purposes.

Your responsibility for screenshots

If you attach a screenshot or paste conversation text into feedback, review it first. Remove names, account details, private prompts, client information, credentials, or other material you do not want to share. You decide what is included and are responsible for having the right to submit it.

Public website and external links

The ReadBooster website is a static GitHub Pages site. It does not include accounts, a payment system, a ReadBooster backend, or analytics or advertising added by this project.

The site links to external services such as the portfolio and Tally. Following an external link takes you to a service with its own terms and privacy practices. ReadBooster is not responsible for the content or privacy behavior of those external sites.

Future policy updates

This policy may be updated when ReadBooster’s capabilities, supported platforms, release process, or data handling changes. A revised policy will be published on this page with an updated effective date. Material changes should be reviewed before using the affected release.

Contact

For a privacy question or correction, use the ReadBooster feedback form and identify the message as a privacy request. You can also reach the project owner through the public portfolio.
