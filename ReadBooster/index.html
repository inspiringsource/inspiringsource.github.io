Create notes directly inside the reading document and keep them connected to the relevant section. Directional indicators show whether saved notes are above or below your current position, making them easy to find even in long conversations.

Privacy by design

The document is built where the conversation already is.

ReadBooster accesses page content locally to render the document view. It does not automatically send your chat content to a ReadBooster server.

  • Conversation formatting happens locally in the browser.
  • Reading fonts, including Fast Reading, are bundled locally with the extension.
  • Preferences, custom section titles, and document notes are stored locally in your browser.
  • Feedback is sent only when you deliberately submit the Tally form.
  • You decide what text or screenshots you include.

ReadBooster FAQ

What does ReadBooster do?

ReadBooster transforms AI conversations into structured documents that are easier to read, navigate, review, annotate with notes, and revisit. It provides continuous and focused reading modes, an outline, clearer sections, table controls, and adaptable reading settings.

Can I take notes inside a ReadBooster document?

Yes. ReadBooster lets you create notes at relevant points in the document. When a note is outside the visible area, directional indicators show whether it is above or below your current position so that you can return to it more easily.

Which AI platforms are supported?

ChatGPT, Google Gemini, and Mistral AI are supported in the current release.

What reading styles does ReadBooster offer?

ReadBooster offers Default, Serif, Dyslexia-friendly, and Fast Reading styles. Fast Reading uses fixation-guided letter emphasis, while text size and spacing can be adjusted independently. These settings change only the local document view and do not modify the original conversation.

Does it send conversations to a server?

ReadBooster accesses the conversation page locally to render its document view. It does not automatically send chat content to a ReadBooster server. Feedback is separate and is submitted only when you choose to complete the Tally form.

Is it free?

The current testing release is free. This is not a permanent pricing commitment, and later availability may change.

Does it modify the original AI conversation?

No. ReadBooster creates a separate reading presentation in the browser. Display choices such as section renaming apply to the ReadBooster view, not to the source conversation.

Can I print or save the document?

Yes. ReadBooster includes Print support, which can also be used with the browser’s Save as PDF option.

Are Mistral AI, Claude, and Perplexity supported?

ReadBooster currently supports ChatGPT, Google Gemini, and Mistral AI.

Claude is planned for the 0.7 milestone, and Perplexity is planned for the 0.8 milestone.
